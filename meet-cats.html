Meet the cats

It's time to meet our cats

Kitty Cat Café is the only cat café in Cluj Napoca and it is home to 11 adorable cats with age ranging from 2 to 12 years old. 4 cats are permanent members of the Kitty Cat café family, while the rest are still looking for their forever homes. All the cats in our cafe are taken good care of, and are fully vaccinated, and neutered. You can come to visit and play with them while enjoying a good cup of coffee or a delicious brunch.

What to expect from the cats

You are up for an incredible experience. Our kitties are generally calm, friendly, and snuggly. In order for everyone to have a good time at the café, please be aware of the following rules: Don’t feed the cats. If you want to give them a treat, consult with the staff. Don’t lift them or disturb them, especially when they are sleeping. We know our cats are very photogenic and Instagram-worthy, but please avoid using flash while taking photos of them. Don’t let children play with the cats unattended
